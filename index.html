The purpose:
  • These workouts are designed to reflect the timeframe in which first responders are required to be at 100% intensity.
  • Behind cancer, heart attack is the leading killer of first responders. It’s up to us to prevent it.
  • The stronger we are, the less likely we are to be injured.
  • Feel and look better.
The workouts:
  • They will not be flashy or technical.
  • They will be between 5 minutes and 15 minutes.
  • The weight will reflect that which we need to safely manage on the job.
  • Workouts will be released every 3rd day (standard Kelly schedule).
  • The required equipment will be fairly standard. Medicine balls, kettlebells, barbell, bumper plates, jump rope, pull-up bar, and a plyo-box. There will also be rowing. If you don’t have access to a Concept 2 rower, ask for a substitute.
  • The workouts are all scalable. I will provide scaling when it is necessary.
  • Please enter your name and a score. This helps me to make better workouts. If you’re opposed to entering a score, enter a name at a minimum please.
